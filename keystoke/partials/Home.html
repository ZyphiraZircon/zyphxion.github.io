Hailing from San Diego, I graduated with a BS in Computer Science: Computer Game Design from the Jack Baskin School of Engineering at the University of California, Santa Cruz. I have been a gamer ever since I was old enough to use my thumbs to play a Space Invaders handheld my mother gave me. My first experience with game development came during high school when I discovered modding tools for Halo PC, allowing me to create all sorts of wacky creations. I have experience with Java, Javascript, HTML5, C#, PHP, C++, and Python. I pride myself on my versatility, being able to take on many tasks of different natures and do them well. I am also big on code efficiency; if there is a way to make something cleaner or run faster, I will find it. I won't quit until a problem is solved.
