Sustain and Defend


This was my first game I created as a game design student at UCSC, in Winter Quarter 2010. For this game, I worked on the sprite art, the music, and the collision code, as well as some of the underlying "resource harvesting" mechanics. This game was written in Microsoft XNA and C#. Looking back on this game, it's really cool to see how far I've come since then.
